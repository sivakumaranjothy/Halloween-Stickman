How to Play

Welcome to Halloween Stickman!

Choosing Difficulty:

Select a difficulty level (e.g., Easy, Medium, Hard)


Guess the hidden word by suggesting letters before the figure is fully drawn (typically within 6 incorrect guesses).


If you choose the hard level, and you get 6 points, take a picture and tag @TrudeauSac, whoever has the fastest time will be put into a raffle. :).

Press Enter to continue game while playing.
